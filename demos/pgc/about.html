Our Strategy

Pacific Grove Capital is an Equity Long/Short fund with opportunistic investments in credit. Target industry focus includes consumer, industrials, TMT, and non-bank financials. The concentration is in “best ideas” with the top 10 long positions typically representing 50-70% of the portfolio. The fund undertakes in-depth fundamental research to find transformative change in high quality businesses within its circle of competence; longs have 50-100% targeted upside over 2-3 years.

The short side focuses on companies being disintermediated by technology or competition or with unsustainable margin structures. Pacific Grove Capital has built an institutional quality business with an 8 person Day 1 team. Managing Partner, Jamie Mendola, is joined by three seasoned investment professionals and four business operations professionals, which include CFO/COO, Controller, Director of IR & Business Development, and Office Manager.

Jamie Mendola is the Founder and Portfolio Manager of Pacific Grove Capital. His past experience includes being Partner at Scout Capital, a Principal at Watershed Asset Management, Goldman Sachs and JP Morgan Chase. He graduated summa cum laude from Binghamton University and a M.B.A. from Stanford’s Graduate School of Business.
